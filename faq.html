Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this community for?

Momentvm is for experienced operators, builders, and professionals across domains. If you've built real things—whether in ops, product, data, finance, or growth—and want to level up while giving back, you'll fit right in.

How is membership approved?

Apply by filling out a short form. If shortlisted, you'll be invited to a 15-minute video chat. We look for curiosity, generosity, and expertise—not just titles or CVs.

Why is it free? What's the catch?

There's no catch. Membership is free during our pilot phase. Our goal is to build a high-signal network that's more valuable than any paid course or agency.

How active do I need to be?

Members are expected to ask at least one question and answer one question per month. No lurkers, no passengers. If you go silent for 90+ days, we'll check in and may remove your access.

What do members get access to?

You'll join a curated private community on Circle with expert AMAs, roundtables, power hours, weekly office hours, and peer support across disciplines.

Can I promote my business or services?

No. This is not a lead-gen platform. Helpful contributions are welcome, but self-promotion and spam are not. Violators will be warned or removed.

What tools do I need to use?

We're currently on Circle.so. You'll also receive occasional updates or invites via email.

Can I invite others?

Yes—but only by sharing the application link. All new members must go through the same vetting process. We're keeping the bar high.

Will this ever be paid?

Maybe. We're exploring low-cost models or sponsorships to cover platform fees, but the priority will always be access, not monetization.

Who's behind Momentvm?

A small group of operators and builders who believe the best way to learn is from each other. If you're reading this, you're early.
